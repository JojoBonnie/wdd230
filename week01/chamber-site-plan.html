Site Purpose

The Cape Town Chamber of Commerce aims to promote local businesses, facilitate networking opportunities, and advocate for economic development within the Cape Town area. It serves as a resource for both local businesses and the community to foster growth and prosperity.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Cape Town Chamber of Commerce?
  • What events are being organized by the chamber this month?
  • Who are the current members of the chamber, and how can I get in touch with them?

Color Schema

The primary color scheme includes a deep blue (#005A9C) for headers and a lighter blue (#007ACC) for navigation and highlights. This palette reflects the vibrant and dynamic nature of Cape Town's coastal environment.

Typography

The website will use Arial for its clean readability and versatility, applied across headings and body text to ensure information is accessible and easy to navigate.
